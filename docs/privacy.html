Privacy Policy for EQ+

Last updated: April 7, 2025

EQ+ does not collect, store, or share any user data. The extension runs entirely on your device and does not transmit any information externally.

1. Information Collection

EQ+ does not collect or access any personal data, browsing activity, or other user-identifiable information.

2. Local Storage

The extension may store your EQ and compressor settings locally on your device to improve user experience. This data is not accessible to anyone but you and is never transmitted.

3. Data Sharing

EQ+ does not share any data with third parties.

4. Changes to This Policy

Any future updates to this privacy policy will be reflected on this page.
